About Nrityanjali

After a series of triumphant shows over the past 12 years, we return with more exciting avenues in the world of dance. Kristu Jayanti College presents to you, a rhythmic culmination of Traditional, Classical, Western, Folk Dance and more at NRITYANJALI.

Nrityanjali is a National Level, inter-collegiate dance competition that serves as a platform for enhancing aesthetic talent. With passion so strong and a fire that fuels the hunger of the soul to express its deepest desires, Nrityanjali provides a platform for narration and expression through the art form of dance. It is our pleasure to invite dancers from all over India to join us on the 20th of February2020, to celebrate this festival and the essence of dance at Nrityanjali.
